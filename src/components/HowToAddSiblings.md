# 👥 How to Add Siblings for Quick Switching

## 📋 Step-by-Step Guide for Caregivers

### Method 1: From Caregiver Dashboard (EASIEST)

1. **Logout from Kid Dashboard**
   - Click the purple 👥 button in top-right
   - Click "Logout"

2. **Login as Caregiver**
   - Click "Caregiver Login"
   - Enter your caregiver credentials

3. **Go to Children Tab**
   - You'll see "Your Children" section
   - Click the **+** (Plus) button

4. **Choose: Create New OR Link Existing**
   - **Create New Child** - Make a brand new account
   - **Link Existing Child** - Connect to a kid account that already exists

5. **Fill in Child Info**
   - Username (for kid to login)
   - Password (simple one kids can remember)
   - Name (display name)
   - Age (optional)

6. **Save**
   - Click "Create Child Account"
   - The new child will appear in your list!

7. **Repeat for Each Sibling**
   - Click + again
   - Add another child
   - Keep going until all siblings are added!

---

### Method 2: From Main Login Page

1. **Logout from Kid Dashboard**

2. **On Login Page, Click "Create Account"**

3. **Select Role: "Kid"**

4. **Enter Info:**
   - Username
   - Password
   - (optional: name, age)

5. **Complete Initial Quiz**
   - The new kid needs to do the setup quiz

6. **Repeat for Other Siblings**

---

## ✅ After Adding Siblings

Now when ANY kid logs in, they can:
- Click the purple 👥 button (top-right)
- See ALL sibling profiles
- Click to instantly switch
- No logout needed!

**Family with 3 kids:**
